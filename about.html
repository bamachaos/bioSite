A Heart for Service

Tammy is a perfect example of someone who always wants to help. This is exemplified in everything she does. She will go out of her way to help our kids, grandkids, or the members of our church. She is the same way at work with her patients and coworkers.

Tammy is someone who values helping others and has built her life around that. From an early age, she had an interest in nursing, serving as a candy striper, which later evolved into her current job as a Labor and Delivery nurse. Church and nursing are the cornerstone of her values, combining helping others and serving the community.

Over time, Tammy has developed a strong connection to serving and community. This interest is more than a pastime—it reflects her approach to problem-solving, learning, and personal growth. According to the interview, one of the most influential moments in her life was graduating from Nursing School, which helped shape her perspective and direction.

Outside of work or daily responsibilities, Tammy spends much of her time with her grandkids, camping, or traveling. These activities highlight qualities such as creating memories and making new relationships. They reveal how she balances personal fulfillment, serving others, and making new friends. Friends and family often describe her as one of the most giving people they know.

Looking forward, Tammy hopes to influence others, whether they be new nurses or new members at her church, that anyone can make a difference, one person at a time. Whether through community projects, relationships, or simply encouraging someone, her focus remains on continued growth and meaningful relationships. This site aims to present an authentic snapshot of who she is, what she cares about, and where she is headed.
